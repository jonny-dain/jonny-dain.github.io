About Me

I am a Change Specialist with a focus on integrating IVR and AI technology to drive innovation and create user-centric solutions at Vodafone. With a keen interest in computing, I have a passion for utilising emerging technology to solve real-world problems and relish the pace of change in the digital world.

Sponsored by Vodafone to study Computer Science, I earned a First-Class Honours degree from the University of Birmingham. Throughout my Digital Technology Partnership with Vodafone, I played a pivotal role in diverse projects, overseeing the digital launch of new product releases, leading long-term initiatives, coordinating consumer-wide sale launches on the UK website, and effectively managing key stakeholders.
